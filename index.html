Senior Scientific Officer

Bio

I am a Senior scientific officer at the Center for Satellite Technology and Development under the National Space Research and Development Agency Nigeria, with 5 years of experience in research and development within the areas of space technologies and applications. I also have over 3 years of professional experience in customer care service

I am passionate about data science, machine learning, and data analysis and have been dedicated to learning these fields for nearly 3 years. I am also proficient in Python programming and data visualization and I possess an intermediate background, enabling me to uncover valuable insights from complex datasets.

With a Masters degree in Information Security, one of my primary goals is to integrate my newfound skills in data science with data security and also space technology applications.
